To Whom It May Concern,

There are people you meet in ministry who leave a permanent mark on you, and Miles Walter Johnson is one of those people. I have known this young man since he was small enough to be running circles around the grown-ups in this building, and watching him grow into who he is today has been one of the great privileges of my time here.

Since 2023, Miles has served as our graphic designer at Saint Matthews Christian Center, and I want people to understand what that meant for us. Before Miles, we were doing the best we could. After Miles, we looked like a real, professional ministry. Every flyer, every announcement, every piece of material he created came back polished and thoughtful. He never treated any assignment as small. He treated every single one like it mattered, because to him, it did.

What I want people to know about Miles before anything else, even before his talent, is the kind of person he is. Yes ma'am, no ma'am, holding doors, remembering names, looking you in the eye and meaning it. That has been Miles since he was a boy in these pews, and it has only grown deeper with time. He carries a respect for people that is genuine all the way through.

And he was always there, early, prepared, and ready. But more than showing up for the work, he showed up for people. He would walk into a room and start checking on everyone around him. How are you doing. How is your family. That kind of care is rare, and every person in this building felt it.

He is talented, yes, but more than that he is good, and goodness like his is hard to come by. I give Miles Walter Johnson my highest and most sincere recommendation without a single reservation. Please feel free to reach out to me directly with any questions.

Warm regards,
Tammy Clement
